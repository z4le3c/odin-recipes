Chicken Quesadillas

Description

Chicken Quesadillas are a scrumptious and satisfying Mexican-inspired dish that's beloved for its cheesy, gooey goodness. Imagine tender pieces of cooked chicken, seasoned to perfection, nestled between two large flour tortillas. These tortillas act as a crispy and golden canvas for a generous layer of melted cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese that blankets the chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Steps

  1. Place one tortilla in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Sprinkle half of the cheese over the tortilla.
  3. Spread the shredded chicken, diced bell peppers, onions, and salsa evenly over the cheese.
  4. Top with the remaining cheese and place the second tortilla on top.
  5. Cook until the bottom tortilla is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side until it's crispy and the cheese is melted.
  6. Slice into wedges and serve with additional salsa if desired.
