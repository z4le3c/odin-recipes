Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its simplicity and bold flavors. It features perfectly cooked spaghetti noodles tossed in a fragrant and flavorful sauce made from garlic and olive oil. The dish is often elevated with a touch of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat and garnished with fresh parsley for a burst of freshness.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil over low heat and add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until the garlic is golden but not browned.
  3. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the garlic-infused olive oil. Season with salt and black pepper.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.
